A Contra Entry is used for internal fund transfers that do not affect the profit/loss of the company. Common examples include:
  • Depositing Cash into a Bank account.
  • Withdrawing Cash from a Bank account.
  • Transferring funds between two Bank accounts.
Access this via Accounting > Contra Entries.
